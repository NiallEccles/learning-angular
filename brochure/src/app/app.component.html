SYDNEY AUSTRALIA

Bondi Beach

One of the most famous beach in Sydney and possibly in Australia. This swimming pool laying right next to the ocean has been already photographed on Instagram so many times that it would be impossible to not visit it while I was in Sydney. It's called Bondi Icebergs Pool the entry fee is $6 for the full day. Even though there is a massive beach next to the pool you can still see that many people are enjoying this more 'private' option.

I was lucky to catch it on hot and shiny day in early Australian Autumn in March. I really liked the vibes around here!
